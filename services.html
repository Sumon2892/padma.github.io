Door Step Delivery

If customers order from their home, they must get it delivered at their home! We exist for providing doorstep delivery. Wherever we claim our coverage - in or out of metro, within or outside Dhaka - we ensure we ourselves are delivering the package directly to the customers' door. We believe that a proper ecommerce delivery solution should offer nothing less. We have a well-trained delivery team, who know ins and outs of their assigned geography. And being in the sector for more than a year have given this team confidene of reaching the Customer in minimum hassle.

Cash on Delivery

Customers love to pay after getting the product delivered! And why not? This gives them the same confidence of buying from a traditional outlet, while sitting at the comfort of his own home! Ecommerce is all about convenience, and its delivery system must be likewise. Without offering a proper Cash on Delivery service, it is impossible for any ecommerce business to grow. We understand this, as well as we understand that the collected money need to reach back the merchant asap. With twice/week merchant settlement in practice, we have an effective CoD service running all over our coverage areas.

Any Size AnyWhere

What size, or which weight doesn't get sold over the internet?? From a pair of ear-rings to a set of dumbbells, from a tiny pillow to a huge bean-bag, from a mobile charger to a 56" LED TV - you cannot be in the ecommerce delivery sector and say 'no' to different sizes or weights. We have a fleet of bi-cycles, motor-cycles and covered vans to cater to any package on behalf of the merchant - all these we do to ensure delivering any size, any weight to customer doorstep!

Return Management

Customers want, or rather deserve, the freedom of choice! Our experience says customers browse and select products online, but the actual 'purchase' happen only after receiving the product in hand! At that moment of truth, some might choose to return the product for whatever reason, and some might want to accept only a part of it. Either way, the delivery system needs to have the capability of handling partial and return packages. And those returns need to reach merchant safely, quickly and with proper reasoning. We offer this exact service for our merchants

Standard Delivery

We offer doorstep delivery service that really works. Our Standard Delivery option has ‘next day’ promise, CoD collection facilities, return management and many more. We offer pickup services from multiple warehouses as well. And, the whole delivery system can be tracked online from anywhere around the globe.

Merchants also can have more visibility and clarification on the overall delivery process, return status or payment status.
